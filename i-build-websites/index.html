I build websites

September 27, 2018

Generally small ones, for local business that require a simple website they can update themselves.

Working in web design and development for the past 18 years,  I’ve worked with businesses of all shapes and sizes from small startups to enterprise clients spanning across many industries and around the globe.

I specialise in delivering small websites powered by a modern content management system,  for a low price. My sites load very quickly on all types of devices.

Perfect package for a small business looking to get online, without all the fuss.

How much?
$500. If it's going to cost more, I'll let you know during our first meeting.
If you would like me to organise the hosting of your site,  add an extra $120 per year.

Hosting?
That's where your website lives. It needs to sit on a computer somewhere. That computer needs to be connected to the internet, so your customers can find it. I can organise all that for you.

What do you get?
For $500, you get a homepage, an about page and a contact page - all setup, ready to go. From there, you can go in and create as many new pages as you want, whenever you want; for free.

I will design, develop and deliver the complete website for you.  All websites are mobile friendly and can be edited by you whenever you want.

I also provide a basic manual, written in plain English, that explains how you can edit and look after your new website.

How long does it take?
After paying the deposit, I normally have everything delivered within 48hrs. If you have more specialised design requirements, maybe a little longer.

What do you need to supply?

  • Your domain name
  • Any logos, images and content that you want online
  • A 50% deposit

Why choose a freelancer?
A freelancer can deliver solutions with fast turnaround times and great support, without all the overhead costs associated with an web agency. 

Do you provide ongoing support & maintenance?
Yes.

What now?
, tell me what you need and when you need it.
